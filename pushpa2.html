Pushpa 2 - The Rule

Pushpa 2: The Rule is the much-awaited sequel to the blockbuster film Pushpa: The Rise. Starring Allu Arjun, this movie continues the intense journey of Pushpa Raj as he rises in power and faces even more dangerous enemies. With powerful performances, gripping action sequences, and massy dialogues, the movie promises to be a cinematic spectacle that fans have been eagerly waiting for.

Main Highlights

  • Starring Allu Arjun in a fierce avatar
  • Directed by Sukumar
  • Music by Devi Sri Prasad
